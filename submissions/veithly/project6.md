## Description / 项目简介

ScanPay is a Sui-native QR payment rail for the world's 600 million small merchants. Print a QR card, take it to a stall, a market, a friend. Scan, sign one PTB, settle in under 2 seconds. 0.1% protocol fee, not 2.9%. Non-custodial: ScanPay never holds funds — the merchant address is encoded directly in the QR. Reviewer flow: open `/app`, pick one of three pre-populated merchant carts (Hanoi bánh mì stall, Lisbon café, Mexico City friend split), click **Pay with Sui** — the trial wallet signs a real Sui PTB and the merchant pad ticks +amount with a verifiable on-chain digest.

ScanPay 是面向全球 6 亿小商户的 Sui 原生扫码支付协议。打印一张二维码，贴在摊位、市场、朋友手心都行。扫码、签一次 PTB、2 秒内到账。协议费 0.1%，不是 2.9%；商户地址直接编码进二维码，ScanPay 不托管任何资金。评审流程：`/app` → 选三个预填购物车之一（河内米粉摊 / 里斯本咖啡馆 / 墨西哥城聚餐 AA），点击 **Pay with Sui**，trial wallet 签发真实 Sui Move 调用，商户面板瞬间 +amount，digest 链上可查。
