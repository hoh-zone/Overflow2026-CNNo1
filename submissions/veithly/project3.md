## Description / 项目简介

PunchPredict is a Sui-native prediction market built around ONE Championship props. Predict the next significant strike, round-3 finish, decision side — all open to any wallet, all settled on-chain in seconds. 0.1% protocol fee to a public treasury, vs the 6–10% vig sportsbooks charge today. Live in-fight markets close in 60-second windows aligned to the bell. Reviewer flow: open `/app`, pick one of three live ONE 178 props, slide a $1–$100 stake, click **Bet on Sui Testnet** — the trial wallet signs a real Move call and your Bet object is minted on-chain, verifiable on suivision.xyz.

PunchPredict 是基于 ONE Championship 拳赛 prop 的 Sui 原生预测市场。预测下一次有效击打、第三回合 KO、判定胜负——任何钱包都能进，链上秒级结算。协议费 0.1%，进入公共金库；对比传统 sportsbook 6–10% 的水钱低两个数量级。开打期间的盘口以 60 秒为一个窗口随回合开关合并/重开。评审流程：`/app` → 选 ONE 178 三个 prop 之一，下 $1–$100，点击 **Bet on Sui Testnet**，trial wallet 签发真实 Move 调用，Bet 对象上链铸造，可在 suivision.xyz 验证。
