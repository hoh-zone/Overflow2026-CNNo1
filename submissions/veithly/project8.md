## Description / 项目简介

SplitRail is a Sui-native payroll & split-bill rail. One Programmable Transaction Block fans a coin out to up to 50 recipients in one digest — payroll, group dinners, DAO grants — and each branch can mint a Receipt object the payee owns. No batch loops, no T+2 finality, no off-chain custody. Reviewer flow: open `/app`, pick one of three pre-populated splits (Friday payroll · 8 contractors, group dinner · 6 friends, DAO grant round · 30 recipients), click **Settle on Sui Testnet**. The trial wallet signs a real Move call; the result includes the verifiable Sui Testnet digest.

SplitRail 是 Sui 原生的工资单/AA 账单轨道。一笔 Move 调用交易把一枚 coin 同时分给最多 50 个收款人——工资、聚餐、DAO grant 都行——每个分支还可以铸造一个收款人独有的 Receipt 对象。没有循环批处理、没有 T+2、没有链下托管。评审流程：`/app` 选三个预填账单之一（周五工资 · 8 名外包 / 聚餐 · 6 个朋友 / DAO grant · 30 名捐助人），点击 **Settle on Sui Testnet**，trial wallet 签发真实 Move 调用，结果显示链上可查的 digest。
