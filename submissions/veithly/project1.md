## Description / 项目简介

InstantRent turns the rental lease into a per-second cash stream on Sui. A tenant scans one QR code to top up the next 24 hours; the landlord watches their balance fill in real time on the dashboard; and at any moment a lender can buy the next 11 months of rent and wire USDC to the landlord today. The landlord gets instant liquidity, the tenant just keeps streaming as before, and the lender holds a transferable forward-rent receipt — all settled in one PTB. Reviewer flow: open `/app`, click **Tenant top-up · 24h** (real Sui Testnet Move call), then click **Sell next 11 months → cash today** to see the lender side mint a forward-rent receipt and pay the landlord USDC instantly.

InstantRent 把租约变成 Sui 上的秒级现金流。租客扫码充值未来 24 小时，房东仪表盘上余额实时上涨；任何时刻贷方都可以一次性买下未来 11 个月的租金、当天把 USDC 打给房东。房东拿到即时流动性，租客保持原状继续 stream，贷方持有可转让的远期租金收据——一笔 Move 调用交易全部结清。评审流程：`/app` → **Tenant top-up · 24h**（真实 Testnet Move 调用）→ **Sell next 11 months → cash today**，亲眼看见 USDC 即时打到房东账户。
