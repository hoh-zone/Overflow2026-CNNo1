## Description / 项目简介

HONK is a Sui-native meme coin launcher with a built-in AI vibe filter. Paste a name, drop an image, get scored 0–10 on three axes (vibe, freshness, aquatic-animal bonus). Score > 5 launches in one PTB; a bonded coin object is minted on Sui Testnet that you can buy and sell against the bonding curve immediately. Bonding curve lives in Move — the creator can't pull the floor. Reviewer flow: open `/app`, pick one of three pre-scored memes ($TRUMPBORK, $GOOSEFI, $SLOPWALRUS), click **LAUNCH ON SUI**. The trial wallet signs a real Move call, the coin object is minted, vibe-check rationale renders inline, end-to-end ≤ 30 seconds.
