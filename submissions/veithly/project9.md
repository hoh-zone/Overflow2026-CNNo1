## Description / 项目简介

SunMint tokenizes rooftop solar panels into yield-bearing Sui objects. Each panel is a `Panel` object with a kW gauge that ticks from a live oracle; buying tokens mints a `Token` object backed by panel ownership; the `DividendPool` shared object accrues USDC every settle tick and pays holders proportionally. Reviewer flow: open the marketplace, see Jose's 3 kW panel in Guadalajara with a live kW gauge, buy 10 tokens, watch the next settle tick dividends into your wallet on Sui Testnet — verifiable on suivision.xyz.

SunMint 把屋顶光伏面板代币化，变成有现金流的 Sui 对象。每块面板是一个 `Panel` 对象，kW 表实时同步链下预言机；买代币会铸造一个 `Token` 对象，份额对应面板所有权；`DividendPool` 共享对象在每次 settle tick 累积 USDC，并按份额发给持有人。评审流程：打开 marketplace，看 Jose 在瓜达拉哈拉的 3kW 面板和实时 kW 表，买入 10 个代币，下一次 settle tick 后 USDC 分红到账 Sui Testnet，digest 链上可查。
