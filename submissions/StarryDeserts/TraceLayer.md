## Description / 项目简介

<!-- 一段话描述你的项目：做什么，为什么重要 / What it does, why it matters -->

AI agents are now writing code, filing PRs, summarizing documents,  and acting on behalf of users. However, this work lacks auditability.  Outputs typically live in ephemeral chat logs that can easily be edited, deleted, or contested, making it impossible to prove to a third party  that an agent generated a specific result. Traditional centralized  logging solutions simply reintroduce the trust issues they were meant to solve.

TraceLayer is a lightweight, decentralized provenance  layer that sits alongside any AI agent runner. It ensures that every  digital artifact produced by an agent is tamper-proof and allows for  verifiable replay in the future.
