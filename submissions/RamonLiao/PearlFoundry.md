## Description / 项目简介

<!-- 一段话描述你的项目：做什么，为什么重要 / What it does, why it matters -->

*   **Business Pain Points**: Traditional bank structured notes require massive capital minimums ($50k–$250k) and lockups, shutting out retail users. DeFi options vaults (DOVs) lacked real vol-surface pricing and audit trails, leading to structural failures.
    *   **Solution**: PearlFoundry is a one-click structured note issuance factory. It enables retail users and KOLs to bundle adjacent strikes and expiries from DeepBook Predict into a tokenised Sui Move `Note` object, whilst pinning term sheets and backtest CSVs permanently to Walrus.
    *   **Key Features**:
        1. **Multi-Leg Composition**: Bundles up to 24 adjacent option legs atomically in a single PTB.
        2. **On-Chain Vol Pricing**: Connects to DeepBook Predict's SVI surface to price range accruals natively.
        3. **Walrus-Backed Term Sheets**: Embeds immutable regulatory-friendly term sheet Blob IDs directly inside the Note object.
        4. **SocialFi Leaderboard**: Ranks issuers by realised APR, enabling copy-minting and referral payouts.
