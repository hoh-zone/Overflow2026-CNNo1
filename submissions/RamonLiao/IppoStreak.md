## Description / 项目简介

<!-- 一段话描述你的项目：做什么，为什么重要 / What it does, why it matters -->

*   **Business Pain Points**: Accessing prediction markets remains friction-heavy for casual fans (wallet setup, SUI/dUSDC acquisition, complex trading terms). Additionally, most Web3 apps fail to build community and gamified user retention, relying strictly on speculation.
    *   **Solution**: IppoStreak is a gamified prediction league on Sui. It offers a zero-friction Google zkLogin and sponsors the user's gas and initial dUSDC faucet tokens. Daily binary picks are settled directly on-chain via DeepBook Predict, allowing users to build streaks and upgrade Seahorse NFTs.
    *   **Key Features**:
        1. **zkLogin Sign-In**: Creates non-custodial wallets via Google OAuth in 3 seconds.
        2. **Gasless Play-to-Earn**: Sponsors the first 10 binary picks, eliminating upfront funding costs.
        3. **Authentic CLOB Settlement**: Direct integration with `predict::mint` for fully transparent leaderboards.
        4. **Mutable Seahorse NFTs**: Mutates badge metadata in-place based on winning streaks; supports KOL team copy-trading.
