## Description / 项目简介

<!-- 一段话描述你的项目：做什么，为什么重要 / What it does, why it matters -->

*   **Business Pain Points**: Institutional liquidity providers (LPs) are hesitant to deploy capital into DeepBook Predict's PLP vaults due to a lack of telemetry. Raw SVI parameters are unreadable, no-arbitrage conditions are unverified, and real-time vault utilisation or withdraw-limit capacities are completely opaque.
    *   **Solution**: Fathom serves as the risk and transparency layer for DeepBook Predict. It renders the on-chain SVI parameters into an interactive 3D volatility surface, validates no-arbitrage boundary conditions, tracks PLP vault telemetry, and publishes attested risk snapshots to Walrus.
    *   **Key Features**:
        1. **Volatility Surface Studio**: 3D strike × expiry visualization using Three.js and Plotly.
        2. **Arbitrage-Free Verification**: Mathematical checks flagging calendar or butterfly arbitrage violations.
        3. **PLP Risk Dashboard**: Real-time tracking of vault utilisation, withdrawal capacities, and asset heatmap.
        4. **Walrus-Attested Snapshots**: Stores immutable, cryptographically verifiable risk reports on Walrus.
