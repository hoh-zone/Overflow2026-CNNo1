## Description / 项目简介

<!-- 一段话描述你的项目：做什么，为什么重要 / What it does, why it matters -->

*   **Business Pain Points**: Managing global Web3 payroll is a fragmented process. Wire transfers are slow and expensive, whilst typical crypto payroll tools merely dump stablecoins into a wallet. Employees must manually transfer and deposit earnings into yield protocols, and auditors struggle to reconcile gross-to-net payouts.
    *   **Solution**: Sluice turns a corporate payroll run into a single PTB on Sui. It automates gross-to-net splits atomically, locking tax withholding in an escrow, and routing net earnings into yield-bearing vaults (Scallop/Navi) based on a ratio configured by the employee via their personal `AllocationCap`.
    *   **Key Features**:
        1. **One-Click Payroll Run**: Atomically executes payroll splits for up to 50 employees in a single transaction block.
        2. **Employee `AllocationCap`**: Grants workers absolute authority over their personal savings/yield ratios.
        3. **On-Chain Audit Trails**: Emits signed compliance events including FX snapshots from Pyth.
        4. **zkLogin Onboarding**: Simplifies hire onboarding with Google auth and sponsored gas fees.
