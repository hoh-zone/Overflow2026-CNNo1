## Description / 项目简介

<!-- 一段话描述你的项目：做什么，为什么重要 / What it does, why it matters -->

*   **Business Pain Points**: Web3 onboarding has a steep learning curve. Requiring novices to write down seed phrases, set up wallets, and acquire SUI tokens for gas kills conversion. Additionally, traditional payments are static, missing opportunities to convert transaction events into long-term customer relationships.
    *   **Solution**: KoiQuest turns the social ritual of red packet gifting into a programmable onboarding funnel on Sui. Leveraging zkLogin and sponsored transactions, users claim stablecoins instantly without setting up a wallet or paying gas, immediately unlocking interactive on-chain quests designed to build customer engagement.
    *   **Key Features**:
        1. **Walletless zkLogin**: Onboards users using Google/Apple accounts in under 60 seconds.
        2. **100% Sponsored Transactions**: Zero gas fees for senders and recipients.
        3. **PTB Quest Engine**: Claims, quest generation, and merchant reward locking are chained atomically in one transaction.
        4. **Walrus Media Integration**: Stores custom red packet skins and merchant assets permanently on Walrus.
