## Description / 项目简介

<!-- 一段话描述你的项目：做什么，为什么重要 / What it does, why it matters -->

*   **Business Pain Points**: Web2 creator platforms (such as Patreon or OnlyFans) extract 10% to 50% in platform fees and hold payouts for weeks. Traditional Web3 routing tools are pull-based, requiring recipients to manually claim and pay gas, and lack automated tax reserving or yield generation capabilities.
    *   **Solution**: SuiSieve turns a creator's address into a programmable revenue routing sieve. Utilizing a single PTB, every incoming payment is split automatically according to a predefined config: payouts are pushed directly to collaborators' wallets, tax reserves are segregated, and the remainder is deposited into yield-bearing DeFi positions.
    *   **Key Features**:
        1. **Atomic Routing**: Splitting, tax reserving, and DeFi yield vault deposits executed in one transaction.
        2. **Capability-Gated Vaults**: Leverages Sui's native ownership model to lock up tax reserves under cold-wallet `TaxCap` authority.
        3. **Push-Based Splits**: Collaborators receive funds immediately without manual claim actions or gas costs.
        4. **Immediate Compounding**: Converts idle creator revenues into interest-bearing assets natively.
