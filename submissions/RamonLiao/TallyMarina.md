## Description / 项目简介

<!-- 一段话描述你的项目：做什么，为什么重要 / What it does, why it matters -->

*   **Business Pain Points**: Enterprise adoption of stablecoins and DeFi is surging, but financial teams struggle with fragmented data formats across exchanges/custodians, mismatching accounting policies (IFRS vs US GAAP), and manual reconciliation close processes. Additionally, traditional subledgers cannot parse Sui's object-centric transaction schemas.
    *   **Solution**: TallyMarina standardises Sui transaction blocks and object states into unified financial events, uses a safe AI assistant (without posting authority) to recommend account classifications, and anchors period-close Merkle roots on the Sui blockchain.
    *   **Key Features**:
        1. **Sui-Native Normalisation**: Converts complex object states into unified financial ledger events.
        2. **AI-Assisted Classification**: Recommends categories and metadata with human-in-the-loop review.
        3. **Double-Entry Journal Engine**: Translates events into balanced debit/credit ledger entries.
        4. **Tamper-Evident Auditing (`AuditAnchor`)**: Cryptographically chains close-period hashes on the Sui blockchain.
