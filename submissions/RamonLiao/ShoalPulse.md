## Description / 项目简介

<!-- 一段话描述你的项目：做什么，为什么重要 / What it does, why it matters -->

*   **Business Pain Points**: Prediction markets like Polymarket are cluttered with non-crypto topics. Crypto-natives lack a specialised venue focused purely on trading narrative momentum, whilst DeepBook Predict lacks a consumer-facing product to drive mass retail volume.
    *   **Solution**: ShoalPulse fuses real-time narrative sentiment with Sui's volatility surface. It aggregates DeFi TVL, social signal, and market probability into an on-chain `AttentionIndex`, translating narrative momentum into curated prediction suggestions routed to DeepBook Predict.
    *   **Key Features**:
        1. **Narrative Heatmap (`AttentionIndex`)**: Dynamic on-chain tracker for crypto-sector momentum.
        2. **Curation-to-Trade Routing**: Curates binary betting suggestions based on narrative trends and routes trades to DeepBook.
        3. **Walrus ProfileNFTs**: Gamifies trading with mutable, evolving Seahorse NFTs stored on Walrus.
        4. **Sub-5-Second Onboarding**: Leverages zkLogin and sponsored gas to remove wallet and funding friction.
