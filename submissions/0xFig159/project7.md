## Description / 项目简介

Walrus StorageOps provides production-grade storage operations and management for Walrus on Sui. It offers blob lifecycle management, storage pricing analytics, replication monitoring, and an operational dashboard.

Walrus StorageOps 为 Walrus on Sui 提供生产级存储运维管理。包括 Blob 生命周期管理、存储价格分析、副本监控和运维仪表盘。
