## Description / 项目简介

Prediction Market Integrity Monitor provides real-time integrity monitoring for prediction markets on DeepBook/Sui. It detects market manipulation, verifies oracle data integrity, and generates Walrus-anchored audit reports.

Prediction Market Integrity Monitor 为 DeepBook/Sui 上的预测市场提供实时诚信监控。检测市场操纵、验证预言机数据完整性，并生成 Walrus 锚定的审计报告。
