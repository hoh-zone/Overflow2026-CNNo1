## Description / 项目简介

DeepBook Market Explainer provides market explanation and analysis for DeepBook on Sui. It uses AI to translate complex on-chain market activity into plain-language summaries, helping users understand market dynamics.

DeepBook Market Explainer 为 Sui DeepBook 提供市场解读与分析。利用 AI 将复杂的链上市场活动转化为通俗易懂的总结，帮助用户理解市场动态。
