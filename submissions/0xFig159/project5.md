## Description / 项目简介

PLP Risk Console provides real-time risk monitoring and management for DeepBook liquidity providers on Sui. It tracks position health, impermanent loss, and market exposure with customizable alerts.

PLP Risk Console 为 Sui DeepBook 上的流动性提供者提供实时风控监控。跟踪头寸健康度、无常损失和市场敞口，支持自定义告警。
