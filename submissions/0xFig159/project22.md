## Description / 项目简介

Open Research Notebook provides collaborative research notebooking on Walrus/Sui. It enables researchers to publish, share, and peer-review scientific notebooks with immutable versioning and citation tracking.

Open Research Notebook 在 Walrus/Sui 上提供协作式研究笔记本。研究人员可发布、分享和同行评审科学笔记本，带有不可篡改的版本控制和引用追踪。
