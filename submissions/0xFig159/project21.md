## Description / 项目简介

Predict Postmortem League provides postmortem analysis and learning for prediction markets. It analyzes past prediction outcomes, identifies patterns of bias and error, and helps market participants improve their forecasting skills.

Predict Postmortem League 为预测市场提供事后分析与学习。分析过往预测结果，识别偏差和错误模式，帮助市场参与者提升预测能力。
